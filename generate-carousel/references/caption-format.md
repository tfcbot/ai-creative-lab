# Caption format

The caption pairs with the cover slide and primes the swipe. Same structure every time.

## Structure

```
🪩 <Cover-slide hook line> ↓

<One-sentence framing of the cultural moment or problem>

<One-sentence reframe / promise of what the carousel teaches>

<The body, mirroring the body slides>

<One sentence on what happens if the reader skips this>

<One sentence on what happens if the reader applies this>

<CTA — comment "X" and we'll send you …>

<Hashtags on a separate line>
```

## Body templates by format

| Format | Body shape |
|---|---|
| 1 — Comparison | "Here's what happens when you test [tool A] vs [tool B] across [N] cases:" then a tight comparison list |
| 2 — Workflow | "Here are the [N] steps:" then a numbered list mirroring the body slides |
| 3 — Reveal | "Here are the [N] details that make it look real:" then a list of categories |
| 4 — Asset launcher | "Here's the prompt structure across [N] verticals:" then a list of vertical-specific lines |
| 5 — Listicle | "Here are all [N]:" then a list with each item on its own line |
| 6 — Product | "[N] applications across [vertical]:" then a list of formats |

## Length

- Whole caption ≤ 2200 characters (Instagram cap including hashtags)
- Body block ≤ 6 list items, each ≤ 80 characters
- Sentences kept short — one idea per line

## Hook line

Must mirror the cover slide headline exactly or paraphrase it tightly. The viewer's eye lands on the first line of the caption while the cover slide is still on-screen — they should reinforce each other.

## CTA pattern

For comment-bait carousels (Format 5 closers and similar):

> Comment "<KEYWORD>" and we'll send you the [resource] with all <N> expanded.

For follow-bait carousels:

> Save this and follow @<handle> for more.

For link-bait carousels:

> Link in bio.

Match the CTA to the closer slide. If the closer says "Comment 'X' and get the prompts," the caption ends with the same instruction.

## Hashtag block

5–8 hashtags on a separate line at the very end. Mix one branded hashtag (the account's wordmark) with topic-specific tags. Do not stack 30 hashtags — they read as engagement-bait and underperform.

## Voice

- Casual, no jargon
- Second-person ("you") not third-person
- One reframe per paragraph, not stacked
- No "Did you know" openers, no "In today's post we'll learn" framing
- The carousel does the teaching; the caption does the framing and the CTA

## Lock pattern

Save the caption to `caption.md` at the project root. Get user approval before submitting to Zernio.
